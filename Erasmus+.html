Erasmus+

  • Posted By : Amine EL ARIBI

A European Union program that provides funding for education, training, youth, and sport activities. It promotes the development of skills and employability, and encourages cooperation and mobility within and beyond the EU. Erasmus+ offers opportunities for individuals to study, train, and gain work experience abroad, and provides funding for organisations to carry out transnational projects in the fields of education, training, and youth.
