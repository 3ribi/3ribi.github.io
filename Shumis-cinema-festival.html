Shumis Cinema Festival

  • Posted By : Amine EL ARIBI
  • |
  • Time : annual event

A cultural and cinematic event that takes place periodically in Larache. The festival showcases films and honours outstanding works in the film industry. It also serves as a platform for presenting independent, narrative, documentary, short, and experimental films that are both Moroccan and international. In addition to all of this, it hosts seminars, exhibitions, and dialogue forums that bring together filmmakers and the general audience.
