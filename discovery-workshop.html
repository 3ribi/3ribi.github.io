Discovery Workshop

trimestery
  • Posted By : Amine EL ARIBI
  • |
  • Time : once every trimester

A project with many parts that aims to enhance the youth's abilities to navigate everyday life. It consists of a series of workshops that are held every three months using non-formal learning tools to build the personal and professional skills of local youth and provide new opportunities. These workshops cover a variety of topics such as graphic design, eco-friendly life hacks, entrepreneurship, art and craft, with a particular focus on Erasmus+ and ESC programs.

Erasmus+ and ESC programs

episode 1:

The first episode focused on discovering Erasmus plus and European Solidarity Corps programs of the European Union. Participants found out what these programs were and learned how to become involved in them. The project aimed to: - Introduce Moroccan young people to both Erasmus+ and European Solidarity Corps programs. - Share the program's opportunities that Moroccan young people could participate in. - Support the participation of Moroccan young people in the Erasmus+ and European Solidarity Corps programs. - Create a platform for exchanging experiences and networking.

Graphic Design Basics: Adobe Illustrator

episode 2:

On the 20th of June, Laracultura organised the second episode of the Discovery Workshop. We explored the basics of graphic design and learned how to use Adobe Illustrator to create visual content, along with other resources useful for graphic design purposes. We were very pleased with everyone's engagement and interest.

Eco-Friendly Lifehacks

episode 3:

The third Discovery Workshop session was entirely eco-friendly, occurring on Saturday, August 14, 2021, at Abdelhamid Zoubair's place. Our special guest guided us through his environmental laboratory. The workshop was divided into three parts, where participants benefited from eco-friendly life-hacks, discovered tools for handmade activities, and gained information about planting to help our mother nature.

Art & Craft

episode 4:

The fourth discovery workshop session was 100% successful, it took place on Thursday, October 28, 2021 at @dart_elfen school, our hosts were super generous with information and good time. The workshop started at 18h40 with a small introduction about Art history and the different tools used in painting, and after that every participant dive-in their artistic sea, expressing their beautiful thoughts.

entrepreneurship and project makin

episode 5:

with Imane Asbai , Abdelmoughit Essafar , and Soufiane Mchichou, all of whom are part of the family of Laracultura. In this workshop we're going to discover together step by step what entrepreneurship is, and how to develop the ability and readiness to create and run a business project.

seasson 2 :

Pottery

episode 1:

the first workshop from the second season of "Discovery Workshop" series, in which this time we discovered pottery and handicraft elements related to it. Pottery craft cooperative in Larache hosted us in their headquarters and other 18 participants to learn and discover the most important techniques of pottery. We discovered through the introductory theoretical workshop the formation stages of clay and its elements, In addition to practical workshop where we learnt pottery making and everyone got the chance to make their own personal embodiment.
