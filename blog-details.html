Chni Banlk

monthly
  • Posted By : Amine EL ARIBI
  • |
  • Time : first sunday of every month

A monthly socio-cultural forum has been established to foster open roundtable discussions on pressing societal issues. These discussions cover a spectrum of sensitive topics including sexual harassment, mental health, youth engagement in politics, the unique challenges faced by women in Morocco, the importance of the right to individuality, and the pervasive issue of racism.

Each session of this open dialogue initiative delves deep into a specific theme, providing a platform for participants to share their perspectives on subjects that often go unaddressed. The varied array of topics, ranging from the personal struggles of individuals to broader societal challenges, creates an inclusive and comprehensive discourse that mirrors the complexities of the world we live in.

Central to the concept is the empowerment of youth, offering them a safe space to express their thoughts and opinions. By addressing issues that directly impact the younger generation, the forum becomes a catalyst for positive change. It serves as a reminder that the voices of the youth are vital in shaping the discourse around these critical topics.

The initiative goes beyond merely discussing issues — it provides a structured platform for participants to articulate their experiences and thoughts. This platform not only validates their voices but also facilitates a constructive exchange of ideas, fostering a community where individuals can find solidarity and support.

In essence, this monthly forum is more than a discussion; it's a building block for a supportive community that acknowledges and confronts societal challenges. By encouraging open conversations on sensitive subjects, it contributes to a more informed and empathetic society, where diverse voices are not only heard but actively contribute to positive social change.
